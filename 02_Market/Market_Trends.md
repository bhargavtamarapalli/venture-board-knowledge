# Market Trends Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Evaluate whether long-term market trends support or weaken the viability of a startup. Trend analysis helps determine if the business is aligned with durable industry shifts rather than temporary hype.

---

## When To Use

- Startup idea validation
- Market opportunity assessment
- Investor due diligence
- Strategic planning
- Product roadmap reviews
- Go-To-Market planning

---

## Inputs Required

- Target industry
- Customer segment
- Geographic scope
- Recent industry reports
- Technology landscape
- Regulatory environment

---

## Core Concepts

Market trends explain how an industry is evolving over time.

Classify trends into:

### Technology Trends
AI, automation, cloud, robotics, etc.

### Customer Trends
Changing behaviors, preferences and purchasing patterns.

### Economic Trends
Macroeconomic conditions affecting demand.

### Regulatory Trends
New policies, compliance requirements and legal changes.

### Competitive Trends
Industry consolidation, disruption and emerging business models.

---

## Framework

1. Identify major trends.
2. Determine whether each trend is short-term or structural.
3. Evaluate impact on customers.
4. Assess impact on business model.
5. Identify opportunities.
6. Identify threats.
7. Recommend strategic actions.

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Trends supported by current evidence
- Customer behavior changing
- Technology adoption increasing
- Regulatory risks understood
- Competitive landscape evolving

---

## Questions To Ask

- Why is this trend happening?
- Will it persist?
- Does it increase demand?
- Does it create new competitors?
- Can the startup benefit?

---

## Metrics

- CAGR
- Adoption Rate
- Technology Maturity
- Market Growth
- Customer Adoption
- Regulatory Risk

---

## Red Flags

- Chasing hype
- Outdated market assumptions
- Ignoring regulatory shifts
- No evidence supporting trends

---

## Best Practices

- Validate trends using multiple sources.
- Focus on structural trends.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Reassess periodically.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming every trend creates opportunity.
- Ignoring customer behavior.
- Confusing news with long-term trends.
- Depending on a single report.

---

## Output Format

Produce:

- Trend Summary
- Opportunity Assessment
- Threat Assessment
- Strategic Implications
- Confidence Level
- Recommended Actions
