# TAM / SAM / SOM Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Evaluate the commercial opportunity of a startup by estimating the Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM). This framework helps determine whether a business opportunity is sufficiently large, realistically reachable, and attractive for investment.

---

## When To Use

- Startup idea validation
- Investor due diligence
- Market opportunity assessment
- Business planning
- Go-To-Market strategy
- Financial projections

---

## Inputs Required

- Target customer segment
- Geography
- Pricing model
- Industry data
- Market reports
- Customer assumptions

---

## Core Concepts

### TAM
Total revenue opportunity if every potential customer adopted the solution.

### SAM
Portion of TAM addressable with the current business model, geography and product.

### SOM
Realistic market share that can be captured within the next 3–5 years.

---

## Framework

1. Define target customer.
2. Estimate total potential customers.
3. Calculate TAM.
4. Narrow to reachable market (SAM).
5. Estimate achievable share (SOM).
6. Validate assumptions using external research.
7. Document calculation methodology.

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Customer segment clearly defined
- Bottom-up estimates preferred
- Assumptions documented
- Geographic scope justified
- Pricing realistic
- SOM achievable

---

## Questions To Ask

- Who can actually buy?
- Which regions are served?
- What constraints reduce the market?
- What share is realistically attainable?

---

## Metrics

- TAM
- SAM
- SOM
- CAGR
- Addressable Customers
- Market Penetration %

---

## Red Flags

- TAM only analysis
- Unrealistic SOM
- Unsupported assumptions
- Ignoring competitive landscape

---

## Best Practices

- Prefer bottom-up sizing.
- Cite live market research.
- Revisit annually.
- Separate facts from assumptions.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Confusing TAM with opportunity.
- Inflating market size.
- Ignoring execution constraints.
- Using outdated reports.

---

## Output Format

Produce:
- TAM Calculation
- SAM Calculation

- Assumptions

- Investment Assessment
