# GTM_Launch.md

# Go-To-Market Launch Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Provide a structured framework for planning, executing and evaluating a product launch. The objective is to maximize customer adoption, reduce execution risk and establish a repeatable launch process.

---

## When To Use

- MVP launch
- New product launch
- Feature launch
- Market expansion
- Investor due diligence
- Commercial release planning

---

## Inputs Required

- Product roadmap
- Target customer
- Positioning
- Marketing plan
- Sales readiness
- Support readiness
- Success metrics

---

## Core Concepts

A successful launch requires coordinated execution across product, marketing, sales, customer success and operations.

The launch process should be outcome-driven, measurable and continuously improved.

Launch phases include:

- Preparation
- Internal Readiness
- External Launch
- Performance Monitoring
- Post-Launch Optimization

---

## Framework

### Step 1 — Define Launch Goals

Specify measurable business outcomes.

### Step 2 — Prepare the Product

Verify product quality, documentation, onboarding and support readiness.

### Step 3 — Align Internal Teams

Ensure marketing, sales and support teams are trained and prepared.

### Step 4 — Execute Launch

Coordinate announcements, campaigns, customer communication and sales activities.

### Step 5 — Measure Performance

Monitor customer adoption, engagement, feedback and revenue.

### Step 6 — Review & Iterate

Conduct a post-launch review and prioritize improvements.

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Product ready
- Target audience defined
- Messaging finalized
- Marketing assets complete
- Sales enabled
- Success metrics documented
- Rollback plan prepared

---

## Questions To Ask

- Is the product ready for customers?
- Are internal teams aligned?
- How will launch success be measured?
- What risks could delay adoption?
- What improvements are planned after launch?

---

## Metrics

- Launch Adoption Rate
- Activation Rate
- Conversion Rate
- Customer Satisfaction
- Revenue Generated
- Support Ticket Volume
- Retention Rate

---

## Red Flags

- Launch without validation
- Poor internal communication
- Missing customer onboarding
- Undefined success metrics
- No contingency plan

---

## Best Practices

- Launch to a limited audience first.
- Monitor metrics daily after release.
- Gather customer feedback immediately.
- Iterate rapidly based on evidence.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Treating launch as the finish line.
- Ignoring onboarding.
- Measuring only downloads.
- Delaying customer feedback collection.

---

## Output Format

Produce:

- Launch Plan
- Readiness Assessment
- KPI Dashboard
- Risk Register
- Post-Launch Review
- Recommendations
