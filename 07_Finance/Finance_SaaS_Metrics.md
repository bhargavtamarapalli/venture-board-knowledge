# SaaS Metrics Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Provide a standardized framework for evaluating the health, scalability, efficiency and financial performance of SaaS businesses. These metrics enable founders and investors to assess growth quality, operational efficiency and long-term sustainability.

---

## When To Use

- Startup idea validation
- SaaS performance reviews
- Investor due diligence
- Board reporting
- Financial planning
- Growth optimization

---

## Inputs Required

- Revenue data
- Customer data
- Subscription information
- Churn data
- Sales and marketing costs
- Product usage metrics

---

## Core Concepts

SaaS businesses are evaluated using recurring revenue and customer economics rather than one-time sales.

Core metric categories:

- Revenue
- Growth
- Customer Economics
- Retention
- Efficiency
- Profitability

Healthy SaaS businesses optimize both growth and capital efficiency.

---

## Framework

### Step 1 — Revenue Metrics

Track:

- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)

---

### Step 2 — Customer Metrics

Measure:

- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Active Customers
- Logo Growth

---

### Step 3 — Retention Metrics

Evaluate:

- Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Customer Churn
- Revenue Churn

---

### Step 4 — Growth Metrics

Monitor:

- MRR Growth
- Expansion Revenue
- Upsell Rate
- Referral Rate

---

### Step 5 — Efficiency Metrics

Review:

- CAC Payback Period
- LTV:CAC Ratio
- Burn Multiple
- Magic Number
- Rule of 40

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Recurring revenue growing
- Healthy gross margins
- Low customer churn
- Strong retention
- Efficient customer acquisition
- Sustainable growth

---

## Questions To Ask

- Is recurring revenue increasing?
- Is churn improving?
- Are customers expanding usage?
- Is CAC recoverable?
- Does growth justify spending?

---

---

## Red Flags

- High logo churn
- Negative revenue retention
- Poor gross margins
- Unsustainable CAC
- Slow payback period

---

## Best Practices

- Review SaaS metrics monthly.
- Focus on retention before acquisition.
- Benchmark against industry standards.
- Improve efficiency continuously.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Tracking vanity metrics.
- Ignoring cohort analysis.
- Measuring revenue without retention.
- Optimizing growth at any cost.
