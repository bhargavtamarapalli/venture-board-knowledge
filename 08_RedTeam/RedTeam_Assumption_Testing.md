# Assumption Testing Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Provide a structured process for identifying, prioritizing, validating and eliminating critical business assumptions before significant time, money and resources are invested. The framework reduces startup risk by replacing beliefs with evidence.

---

## When To Use

- Startup idea validation
- MVP planning
- Investor due diligence
- Product strategy
- Go-To-Market planning
- Business model validation

---

## Inputs Required

- Business model
- Customer interviews
- Product hypothesis
- Market research
- Financial assumptions
- Competitive analysis

---

## Core Concepts

Every startup is built on assumptions.

The highest-risk assumptions should always be tested first.

Assumptions typically fall into:

- Problem Assumptions
- Customer Assumptions
- Solution Assumptions
- Pricing Assumptions
- Market Assumptions
- Growth Assumptions
- Operational Assumptions

Validation requires observable evidence rather than opinions.

---

## Framework

### Step 1 — List Assumptions

Document every critical assumption supporting the business.

---

### Step 2 — Prioritize

Rank assumptions using:

- Business Impact
- Uncertainty
- Ease of Validation

Test high-impact, high-uncertainty assumptions first.

---

### Step 3 — Design Validation Experiments

Examples include:

- Customer interviews
- Landing pages
- Concierge MVPs
- Smoke tests
- Paid advertisements
- Prototype testing

---

### Step 4 — Execute Experiments

Collect measurable evidence.

Document both positive and negative outcomes.

---

### Step 5 — Decide

Classify each assumption as:

- Validated
- Invalidated
- Requires More Evidence

Update business strategy accordingly.

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Assumptions documented
- Prioritization completed
- Experiments designed
- Evidence collected
- Decisions recorded
- Learnings shared

---

## Questions To Ask

- What must be true for this business to succeed?
- Which assumption carries the highest risk?
- What evidence already exists?
- What is the fastest validation method?
- What decision depends on this assumption?

---

## Metrics

- Assumptions Tested
- Validation Rate
- Invalidated Assumptions
- Experiment Cycle Time
- Learning Velocity
- Evidence Quality

---

## Red Flags

- Untested assumptions
- Confirmation bias
- Building before validation
- Ignoring contradictory evidence
- Long validation cycles

---

## Best Practices

- Test assumptions continuously.
- Seek disconfirming evidence.
- Keep experiments inexpensive.
- Make decisions based on data.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Treating opinions as evidence.
- Testing multiple assumptions simultaneously.
- Ignoring failed experiments.
- Optimizing without validation.

---

## Output Format

Produce:

- Assumption Register
- Validation Experiments
- Evidence Summary
- Validation Status
- Risks
- Recommendations
