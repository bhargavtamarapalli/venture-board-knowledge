# Red Team Risk Assessment Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Provide a structured methodology for identifying, evaluating and mitigating the highest-impact risks that could prevent a startup from achieving sustainable success. This framework enables objective decision-making by challenging assumptions and exposing critical weaknesses before significant resources are committed.

---

## When To Use

- Startup idea validation
- Investor due diligence
- Product launch readiness
- Business model review
- Strategic planning
- Fundraising preparation

---

## Inputs Required

- Business model
- Product strategy
- Market research
- Financial model
- Customer validation
- Competitor analysis
- Technical architecture

---

## Core Concepts

Risk assessment evaluates uncertainty across every major area of the startup.

Risk categories include:

- Market Risk
- Customer Risk
- Product Risk
- Technology Risk
- Financial Risk
- Operational Risk
- Legal & Regulatory Risk
- Competitive Risk
- Execution Risk

Every identified risk should be evaluated for:

- Probability
- Impact
- Detectability
- Mitigation Strategy
- Owner

---

## Framework

### Step 1 — Identify Risks

List all material risks across each category.

### Step 2 — Assess Probability

Classify each risk as:

- Low
- Medium
- High

---

### Step 3 — Assess Business Impact

Estimate potential effect on:

- Revenue
- Customers
- Reputation
- Operations
- Funding

---

### Step 4 — Define Mitigation

Document preventive actions, contingency plans and validation experiments.

---

### Step 5 — Prioritize

Rank risks using a risk matrix considering probability and impact.

Focus first on high-probability, high-impact risks.

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Major risk categories covered
- Risks supported by evidence
- Probability documented
- Impact assessed
- Mitigation identified
- Ownership assigned

---

## Questions To Ask

- What assumptions could invalidate the business?
- Which risks threaten survival?
- Can the risk be reduced through validation?
- What early warning signals exist?
- What contingency plans are available?

---

## Metrics

- Number of Critical Risks
- High-Risk Count
- Risk Exposure Score
- Mitigation Coverage
- Validation Progress
- Residual Risk

---

## Red Flags

- No documented risks
- Optimistic assumptions only
- Missing mitigation plans
- Ignoring external threats
- Undefined ownership

---

## Best Practices

- Review risks regularly.
- Validate assumptions early.
- Quantify risk whenever possible.
- Maintain a living risk register.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Confusing uncertainty with low risk.
- Ignoring execution risk.
- Treating risk assessment as a one-time activity.
- Failing to prioritize mitigation.

---

## Output Format

Produce:

- Risk Register
- Risk Matrix
- Priority Risks
- Mitigation Plan
- Residual Risk Assessment
- Recommendations
