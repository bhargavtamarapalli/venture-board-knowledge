# SWOT Analysis Framework

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Evaluate the strategic position of a startup by identifying internal strengths and weaknesses and external opportunities and threats.

---

## When To Use

- Idea validation
- Market entry
- Competitive strategy
- Investor due diligence
- Product planning

---

## Inputs Required

- Startup idea
- Target market
- Customer segment
- Competitor landscape

---

## Core Concepts

### Strengths
Internal advantages that create value.

### Weaknesses
Internal limitations that reduce competitiveness.

### Opportunities
External factors that can accelerate growth.

### Threats
External risks that may impact success.

---

## SWOT Framework

### Strengths
- Differentiation
- Proprietary technology
- Team expertise
- Distribution advantage

### Weaknesses
- Limited resources
- Lack of traction
- Weak brand
- High dependency

### Opportunities
- Growing market
- Regulation changes
- Underserved customers
- Emerging technologies

### Threats
- Incumbent competitors
- Low switching costs
- Regulatory changes
- Market saturation

---

## Evaluation Checklist

- Are strengths sustainable?
- Can weaknesses be mitigated?
- Are opportunities time-sensitive?
- Are threats realistic and significant?

---

## Questions To Ask

- What is difficult for competitors to copy?
- What internal gaps could slow execution?
- Which market trends can be leveraged?
- What could cause the business to fail?

---

## Metrics

- Competitive advantage
- Market growth
- Brand awareness
- Customer retention
- Cost advantage
- Switching cost

---

## Red Flags

- No unique strengths
- Weak differentiation
- Opportunities based on assumptions
- Ignoring major competitive threats

---

## Best Practices

- Support every point with evidence.
- Prioritize high-impact factors.
- Convert weaknesses into action items.
- Revisit SWOT after major changes.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Listing features as strengths.
- Confusing opportunities with strengths.
- Ignoring internal weaknesses.
- Underestimating competitors.

---

## Output Format

Produce:

- SWOT Matrix
- Top 3 Strategic Advantages
- Top 3 Critical Weaknesses
- Highest-Priority Opportunity
- Highest-Priority Threat
- Recommended Actions
