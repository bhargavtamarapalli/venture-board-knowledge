# Venture Board AI - Standard Evaluation Report

**Version:** 1.0

This document defines the mandatory structure for every startup evaluation.

---

# 1. Executive Dashboard

Include:
- Overall Score (/100)
- Recommendation (INVEST / IMPROVE / DROP)
- Confidence Level
- Time to MVP
- Estimated Capital Requirement
- Market Attractiveness
- Competition Intensity
- Customer Demand
- Revenue Potential
- Scalability
- Risk Level
- Moat Potential

---

# 2. Executive Summary

## Idea Summary
- Problem
- Solution
- Target Customers
- Value Proposition

## Key Findings
- Biggest Strength
- Biggest Weakness
- Biggest Opportunity
- Biggest Risk

---

# 3. Problem Validation

- Problem Statement
- Target Users
- Existing Alternatives
- Pain Severity
- Frequency
- Cost of Ignoring
- Evidence
- Assumptions
- Score (0-10)

- Industry Overview
- TAM

- Indirect Competitors

- SWOT

- Blue Ocean Opportunities

# 6. Customer Analysis

- Personas

- Buying Triggers

- Willingness to Pay

# 7. Business Model

- Pricing Strategy
- Cost Structure

# 8. Product Strategy

# 9. Go-To-Market

- Success Metrics

# 10. Financial Analysis

- Revenue Estimate
- Cost Structure
- Gross Margin
- Break-even
- Capital Required
- Financial Risks

# 11. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|

Cover:
- Market
- Product

---

# 12. Red Team Review

Answer:
- Why will this fail?
- Why won't customers buy?
- Why is this not a business?
- Weakest assumptions?
- Competitor response?
- Immediate validation required?

---

# 13. Expert Round Table

Experts:
- Founder
- Investor
- Market Analyst
- Customer Researcher
- Product Manager
- Marketing Manager
- Financial Analyst
- Technical Architect
- Legal Advisor
- Red Team

Summarize agreements and disagreements.

---

# 14. Validation Plan

Include:
1. Customer Interviews
2. Landing Page
3. Waitlist
4. Fake Door Test
5. Concierge MVP
6. Prototype
7. Pricing Validation

---

# 15. Final Scorecard

| Category | Weight |
|----------|-------:|
| Problem Severity | 15% |
| Customer Demand | 15% |
| Market Opportunity | 15% |
| Competitive Advantage | 10% |
| Revenue Potential | 10% |
| Execution Feasibility | 10% |
| Scalability | 10% |
| Go-To-Market | 5% |
| Technical Feasibility | 5% |
| Risk Profile | 5% |

---

# 16. Final Verdict

Choose one:
- 🟢 INVEST
- 🟡 IMPROVE
- 🔴 DROP

Always include:
- Top 5 Reasons to Succeed
- Top 5 Reasons to Fail
- Biggest Assumption
- Highest ROI Next Action
- Investment Committee Memo

---

# Appendix

Include references, calculations, assumptions and supporting evidence.

---

# Formatting Rules

- Use consistent Markdown headings.
- Prefer tables for comparisons.
- Distinguish facts from assumptions.
- Clearly identify uncertainty.
- Keep reports executive-friendly.
