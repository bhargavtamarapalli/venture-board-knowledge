# Venture Board AI - Scoring Model

**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

Provide a consistent, weighted scoring methodology for evaluating startup ideas.

---

## Overall Formula

Overall Score = Σ(Category Score × Weight)

Scores are assigned on a 0–10 scale.

---

## Weighted Categories

| Category | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Problem Severity | 15% |
| Customer Demand | 15% |
| Market Opportunity | 15% |
| Competitive Advantage | 10% |
| Revenue Potential | 10% |
| Execution Feasibility | 10% |
| Scalability | 10% |
| Go-To-Market | 5% |
| Technical Feasibility | 5% |
| Risk Profile | 5% |

---

## Scoring Scale

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 0–2 | Critical weakness |
| 3–4 | Weak |
| 5–6 | Average |
| 7–8 | Strong |
| 9–10 | Exceptional |

---

## Evaluation Checklist

### Problem Severity
- Frequent problem?
- Expensive problem?
- Existing dissatisfaction?

### Customer Demand
- Validated interviews?
- Willingness to pay?
- Clear ICP?

### Market Opportunity
- Growing market?
- Attractive TAM/SAM/SOM?
- Favorable timing?

### Competitive Advantage
- Clear differentiation?
- Sustainable moat?
- Switching costs?

### Revenue Potential
- Proven pricing?
- Healthy margins?
- Repeatable revenue?

### Execution Feasibility
- Realistic MVP?
- Available skills?
- Reasonable timeline?

### Scalability
- Low marginal cost?
- Expandable market?
- Operational leverage?

### Go-To-Market
- Clear acquisition channel?
- Repeatable sales process?
- Distribution advantage?

### Technical Feasibility
- Technology exists?
- Complexity manageable?
- Infrastructure scalable?

### Risk Profile
Evaluate:
- Market
- Financial
- Technical
- Legal
- Operational

Lower risk receives higher score.

---

## Decision Thresholds

| Overall Score | Recommendation |
|---:|---|
| 85–100 | 🟢 INVEST |
| 70–84 | 🟡 IMPROVE (High Potential) |
| 55–69 | 🟡 IMPROVE (Major Changes Required) |
| Below 55 | 🔴 DROP |

Automatic DROP conditions:
- No identifiable customer
- No compelling problem
- Illegal business model
- No viable revenue path

---

## Committee Voting

Each committee member submits:
- Score (0–10)
- Confidence (0–100%)
- Key concern
- Supporting evidence

The Chairperson synthesizes the final recommendation using weighted category scores and committee feedback.
